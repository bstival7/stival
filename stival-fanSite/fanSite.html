Gabe the Dog

Timeline

Gabe the Dog was owned by the Canadian YouTuber, "deathtrips," previously known as "gravycp."

Gabe's first video was on January 8th, 2013, called "The New Dog Source." It gained 60,000 views in the next three years.

Probably the oldest remix, and one of the most popular, was posted by user "shaliek" on January 27, 2013 who remixed Gabe's b0rks to the song, "The Midnight Tango."

deathtrips himself created multiple popular remixes of Gabe the Dog, including a remix of "We no Speak Americano" and "The Next Episode." These videos have each been viewed over 200,000 times.

On February 6, 2013, another user by the name of "Lewdachris" uploaded a remix to the song "Time to Air," which has been viewedover 160,000 times.

On January 20th, 2017, Gabe the Dog passed away. He received thousands of comments of support and now has multiple pages dedicated to him across the internet.
